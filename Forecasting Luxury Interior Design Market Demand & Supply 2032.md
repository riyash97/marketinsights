Key Market Drivers Propelling Growth

Market Challenges Restraining Expansion
Despite its promising growth, the luxury interior design market faces some challenges that could impact its momentum:
•	High Costs of Luxury Design Solutions: The premium pricing of materials, furniture, and customized design services limits market reach, particularly in developing regions.
•	Skilled Labor Shortage: A lack of experienced designers and craftsmen can hinder the execution of intricate luxury projects.
•	Economic Fluctuations: Market downturns and economic uncertainties may lead to reduced spending on high-end interior solutions.
