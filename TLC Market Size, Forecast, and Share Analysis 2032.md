Key Drivers of the Thin Layer Chromatography Market
•	Rising Demand in Pharmaceutical Research: TLC is extensively used in drug discovery and development, making it indispensable for pharmaceutical companies.
•	Food Safety Regulations: Stringent food safety norms worldwide are boosting the adoption of TLC for detecting contaminants and adulterants.
•	Forensic Applications: TLC's ability to analyze complex mixtures is driving its use in forensic laboratories for drug testing and toxicology studies.
•	Cost-Effectiveness: Compared to other chromatography techniques, TLC is more affordable, making it accessible to small and medium-sized enterprises.
Market Restraints
Despite its advantages, the TLC market faces challenges such as the lack of skilled professionals and the emergence of advanced alternatives like High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC). Additionally, the limited sensitivity of TLC in detecting trace amounts of substances can hinder its adoption in certain applications.
