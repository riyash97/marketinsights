Restraints Impacting Market Growth
While the Cocoa Seed Extract Market shows significant promise, there are a few challenges that could hinder its growth. This makes the product relatively expensive compared to other botanical extracts, limiting its use in mass-market products.

Moreover, the reliance on cocoa beans, a crop vulnerable to climate change, could pose supply chain challenges and price volatility.
