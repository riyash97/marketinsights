Key Drivers of the RGP Lens Sales Market
The RGP lens market is propelled by several factors:
•	Increasing Prevalence of Refractive Errors: With over 2.2 billion people globally suffering from vision impairment, the demand for effective corrective lenses is surging.
•	Technological Advancements: Innovations in lens materials and designs are enhancing comfort and performance, making RGP lenses a preferred choice.
•	Growing Awareness About Eye Health: Rising consumer awareness about the benefits of RGP lenses, such as better oxygen flow and reduced risk of eye infections, is boosting adoption.
•	Aging Population: The global aging population, particularly in regions like North America and Europe, is driving demand for advanced vision correction solutions.
Restraints Challenging Market Growth
Despite its promising outlook, the RGP lens market faces certain challenges:
•	High Cost: RGP lenses are relatively more expensive than soft lenses, which may deter price-sensitive consumers.
•	Adaptation Period: The initial discomfort and longer adaptation period associated with RGP lenses can discourage potential users.
•	Competition from Soft Lenses: The widespread availability and comfort of soft contact lenses pose a significant challenge to RGP lens adoption.
