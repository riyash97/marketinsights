Market Drivers Fueling Growth
✔ Growing Health Consciousness – Consumers are shifting toward natural, low-calorie, and functional beverages with added health benefits, boosting demand for cranberry juice.
✔ Rising Incidences of Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs) – The increasing cases of UTIs, kidney-related ailments, and digestive disorders have heightened awareness of cranberry juice as a preventive remedy.
✔ Expansion of Organic and Sugar-Free Variants – Leading manufacturers are launching organic, unsweetened, and fortified cranberry juice variants, catering to diabetic and health-conscious consumers.
Challenges Hindering Market Growth
•	High Production Costs – The seasonal nature of cranberries, limited availability, and high processing expenses contribute to increased production costs.
•	Fluctuations in Raw Material Supply – Adverse weather conditions and supply chain disruptions impact cranberry farming and juice production.
•	Taste Preference and Consumer Acceptance – The tart taste of cranberry juice may not appeal to all consumers, limiting its market penetration compared to other fruit juices.
