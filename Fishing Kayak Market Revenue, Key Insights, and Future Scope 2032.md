Key Market Drivers
•	Increasing Popularity of Kayak Fishing: The surge in interest in fishing as a recreational sport is driving demand for specialized fishing kayaks, particularly among hobbyists and professional anglers.
•	Eco-Friendly and Cost-Effective Alternative to Boats: Unlike motorized fishing boats, kayaks offer an environmentally sustainable and affordable fishing experience, attracting nature-conscious consumers.
•	Technological Advancements in Kayak Design: Innovations such as pedal-driven propulsion, hands-free steering, and built-in fishing accessories are enhancing user experience and boosting sales.

•	High Initial Investment for Premium Models: While basic fishing kayaks are affordable, advanced models with premium features can be expensive, limiting their accessibility to budget-conscious consumers.
