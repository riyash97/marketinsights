Market Drivers
The coated duplex board market is primarily driven by the following factors:
•	Rising Demand for Sustainable Packaging: With increasing environmental concerns, industries are shifting toward recyclable and biodegradable materials. Coated duplex boards, being eco-friendly, are witnessing heightened demand.
•	Growth in E-commerce: The booming e-commerce sector requires durable and lightweight packaging solutions, boosting the adoption of coated duplex boards.
•	Cost-Effectiveness: Compared to other packaging materials, coated duplex boards offer a cost-efficient solution without compromising on quality.
•	Versatility in Applications: These boards are widely used in food packaging, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods due to their excellent printability and durability.
Market Restraints
Despite its growth potential, the market faces certain challenges:
•	Fluctuating Raw Material Prices: Volatility in the prices of raw materials like pulp and paper can impact production costs.
•	Stringent Environmental Regulations: Compliance with stringent environmental norms can increase operational costs for manufacturers.
•	Competition from Alternatives: Materials like plastic and corrugated boards pose a threat to the market growth of coated duplex boards.
