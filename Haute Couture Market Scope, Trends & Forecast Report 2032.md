Challenges Impacting Market Growth
•	High Production Costs – Crafting customized, hand-sewn garments with premium materials significantly raises production expenses.
•	Limited Consumer Base – Unlike ready-to-wear fashion, haute couture targets an exclusive, niche clientele, restricting mass-market penetration.
•	Sustainability Concerns – The fashion industry's environmental impact has prompted scrutiny, leading brands to adopt eco-friendly materials and ethical sourcing practices.
